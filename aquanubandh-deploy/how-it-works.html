THE WORKFLOW

How Aquanubandh Works

A step-by-step guide to managing a digital Vannamei cycle.

1

Stocking & Setup

Every cycle starts with clear data. Log your PL count, origin hatchery, and initial water parameters. The app will immediately establish a baseline for your pond, allowing it to accurately predict survival rates as the cycle progresses.

2

Daily Operations

Your field workers use the app to log the exact amount of feed given across four daily sessions (Morning, Noon, Afternoon, Evening). Because the app works offline, poor network coverage on the farm is never an issue. Data syncs automatically when reconnected.

3

Mid-Cycle Sampling

Once you hit DOC 30+, simply enter your weekly sampling data (ABW). The app instantly transitions from DOC-based survival assumptions to highly accurate ABW-based calculations, updating your total pond biomass in real-time.

4

Harvest & Real-time Profit

As you approach harvest, the app provides your exact, calculated FCR. Compare your crop against the built-in live market prices to determine the perfect day to sell, maximizing your profit margins.
